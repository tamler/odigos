How we think

Principles that guide every engagement.

01
Memory is the product. An AI system that forgets is just a chatbot. Every interaction should make the next one better.
02
Actions over words. The system should do things, not describe how to do things. We build AI that takes action.
03
Resilience over perfection. It's better to degrade gracefully than crash spectacularly. Self-healing beats self-optimizing.
04
Lean core, rich periphery. Complexity belongs in modular tools and skills, not in the brain. Keep the core simple.
05
Your data, your server. Nothing phones home. No vendor lock-in on critical infrastructure. You own what we build.
06
Don't over-engineer. If a simpler approach works, use it. We add complexity when we hit the problem, not before.
